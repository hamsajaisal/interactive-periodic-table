The Interactive Periodic Table

Explore all 118 elements by arrow key, search, or touch. Every element announces its full identity as you move, and a detailed card is one keystroke away.

Press Enter to focus the element. Press Shift+Enter to open its full details.

Keyboard shortcuts & how to explore
↑ ↓ ← → — Move between elements in the grid
Home / End — First / last element in the current period
Enter or Space — Open the element's detail card
G — Hear where you are: row, group and period
Shift+Enter (in search) — Open details for a typed symbol
Double-click / double-tap an element — Open its detail card
Esc — Close the detail card

As you arrow across the table, your screen reader announces the element's name, symbol, atomic number, group, period and category. At the edge of a period or group you'll hear a note that there are no more elements in that direction. On a phone or tablet, use the round arrow buttons in the corner to move, and the centre button to hear your position.
